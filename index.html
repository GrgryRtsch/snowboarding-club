The Laquionie Snowboarding Club
About us:

The Snowboarding Club is a great way to find out about the latest things happening in and around these beautiful mountains and make new friends.

Who can join?
Info:

Anyone can join, most are from the area of Laquionie but anyone is welcome to join our club we merely want to link people together who have passion for the sport. !

Do I need my own stuff?
Info:

It would be ideal but there are board shops where you can rent stuff also! We also have some spare stuff. And fellow members are a great source of hand-me-downs.
